Caculates the required trajectory of a projectile based on the x and y position of the target and the intial angle

Formulas used:

Velocity:

\[ v=\sqrt{\frac{gp_{x}}{2\cos\left(a\right)\left(\sin\left(a\right)-\frac{\left(p_{y}\cos\left(a\right)\right)}{p_{x}}\right)}} \]

Parameters
pxthe x distance to the target
pythe y distance to the target
anglethe initial angle of the projectile
Returns
The trajectory(angle and velocity) that the projectile must take to reach (px, py)

Formulas Used:

Slope of entry

\[ s_{e}=\tan\left(-a_{e}\right) \]

Velocity in the x direction:

\[ v_{x}=\sqrt{\frac{gp_{x}}{2\left(\frac{p_{y}}{p_{x}}-s_{e}\right)}} \]

Velocity in the y direction:

\[ v_{y}=\frac{\left(p_{y}+\frac{1}{2}g\left(\frac{p_{x}}{v_{x}}\right)^{2}\right)}{\frac{p_{x}}{v_{x}}} \]

Required angle:

\[ a=\arctan\left(\frac{v_{y}}{v_{x}}\right) \]

Required Velocity:

\[ v=\sqrt{v_{x}^{2}+v_{y}^{2}} \]

Parameters
pxthe x position to the target
pythe y position to the target
entryAnglethe desired angle at which the projectile should enter the target
Returns
The trajectory the projectile needs to follow to meet the provided criteria
